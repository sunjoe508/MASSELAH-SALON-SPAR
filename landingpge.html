About This Project

The inspiration for this project stemmed from the need to modernize how beauty services are accessed and booked. After observing operations at a local salon, I realized the potential for a more efficient system that could enhance customer experiences while streamlining management tasks. This project, part of my portfolio at Holberton School, aims to bridge that gap.
